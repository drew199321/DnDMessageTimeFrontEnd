Scrambler 1:

Letters of the string are scrambled with the rest of the string
preserving spaces, meaning that the number of characters per word is the same but the letters may not belong to that word.

Letters of the string are scrambled within their own word, with spaces preserved.

Scrambler 3:

Letters are not rearranged but instead each letter is randomly replaced
with another random letter. This becomes almost impossible to unscramble at high percentages
